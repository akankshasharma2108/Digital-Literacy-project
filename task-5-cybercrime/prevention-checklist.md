## **Part B – Stay Safe Online Checklist**

1. Never click links in messages or emails from unknown sources.  
2. Verify UPI payment requests directly with the sender before paying.  
3. Avoid entering UPI PINs or passwords on third-party websites.  
4. Enable two-factor authentication (2FA) on bank and social accounts.  
5. Regularly update apps and operating systems to fix security vulnerabilities.  
6. Use strong, unique passwords and a password manager.  
7. Do not share personal information like Aadhaar, PAN, or bank details publicly.  
8. Report suspicious activity to the **National Cyber Crime Portal: [cybercrime.gov.in](https://www.cybercrime.gov.in)** or call **Helpline 1930**.
